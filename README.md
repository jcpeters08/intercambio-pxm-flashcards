# Intercambio PXM — Flashcards: *The Book of Life*

Bilingual Spanish/English flashcards for the language exchange class in Puerto Escondido.

**Live:** https://jcpeters08.github.io/intercambio-pxm-flashcards/

## Content
30 vocabulary terms drawn from the film *The Book of Life* (2014), grouped into three segments:

1. **La Apuesta** — the wager between La Muerte and Xibalba
2. **La Corrida de Toros** — the bullfight
3. **Los Recordados** — the Land of the Remembered

## Features
- **Study mode:** flip cards, swipe or arrow through the deck, mark ★ Mastered or ↻ Still learning
- **Quiz mode:** multiple-choice with score tracking, optional timer, auto-tags mastered/learning based on answers
- **Results screen:** side-by-side split of what you know vs. what needs work, plus a "Quiz still learning" button to drill only the missed cards
- **Filter by segment** (1/2/3) or by **status** (mastered/learning)
- **Direction toggle:** ES → EN or EN → ES
- **Mobile-friendly:** swipe gestures, responsive layout
- **Keyboard shortcuts:** ← → to navigate, Space to flip, M/L to mark, A–D or 1–4 to answer quiz

## Tech
Single-file `index.html` — no build step, no dependencies, no tracking. Runs anywhere.
